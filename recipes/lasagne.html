Lasagne

Description

This recipe will show you how to make an Authentic Italian lasagne

Ingredients

  • Lasgne sheet
  • Bolognase
  • White Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Add layer of bolognase to dish
  3. Add layer of white sauce
  4. Add layer of lasagne sheets
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 3 times
  6. Add final layer of Cheese
  7. Cook for 40 minutes
